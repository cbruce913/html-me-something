What are Marble Olympics?!

Marble Olympics is a collection of 16 tests of feat, endurance, and skill. 16 teams participate to see who will come out on top.

Presentng the teams.

Team Galactic

Team Galactic hails from the Western Asian city of Galakonur, renowned for its scientific advancement, especially in astronomy. They are the host team of this year's Marblelympics. They are a team of transparent silver and white marbles. This is their 5th time making it to the Marblelympics and though they have yet to take home the coveted Marble League Trophy; if they can manage it this year the victory will be sweetened by the knowledge it was done at home.
